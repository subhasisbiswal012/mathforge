About MathForge

MathForge was built because rote memorization is boring — but timed pressure makes it stick. The idea is simple: flashcard-style questions, a countdown timer, and a brutally honest results screen at the end.

Every topic is a self-contained module. Adding a new topic takes minutes — just one object in topics.js. No frameworks, no build step, no backend. Pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
